What is Bioinformatics?

Posted on June 10, 2025

Bioinformatics is the intersection of biology, computer science, and data analysis...

It enables researchers to work with massive datasets from genome sequencing, transcriptomics, proteomics, and more — extracting meaningful patterns, biomarkers, and biological insights.

Why It Matters

  • Identifies disease-associated variants
  • Supports personalized medicine
  • Improves our understanding of biology at scale

Tools You’ll Hear About

Common languages: Python, R

Common tools: samtools, bcftools, DESeq2, GATK, Nextflow, Snakemake

What I’ll Be Sharing

This blog will explore practical workflows, data QC tips, and real-world genomics problems. Whether you're a beginner or researcher, I hope you'll find value here!
