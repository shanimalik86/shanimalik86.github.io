🧬 What is Bioinformatics?

Posted on June 10, 2025

Bioinformatics is the interdisciplinary field that develops methods and software tools to understand biological data. It plays a key role in modern biology and medicine, especially with the rise of big data from genomics and next-generation sequencing (NGS) technologies.

Why Start a Bioinformatics Blog?

  • 🧠 To share technical knowledge from real-world pipelines and analyses.
  • 🔍 To explain commonly used tools and techniques like samtools, bcftools, and Snakemake.
  • 🌍 To contribute to open science and make workflows reproducible and accessible.

Stay tuned for tutorials, tool reviews, and insights from my experience in clinical genomics and research bioinformatics!
